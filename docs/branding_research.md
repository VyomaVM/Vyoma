# Branding & Identity Research

## 1. Project Naming Concepts

### Option A: IGNITE (The "Energy" Theme) 🧨
*   **Concept**: Explosive speed, starting something powerful, clean energy.
*   **Vibe**: Fast, dangerous but controlled.
*   **Ecosystem**:
    *   **CLI**: `ign` (Short, sharp).
    *   **Daemon**: `ignited` (Always burning).
    *   **Config**: `Ignitefile`.
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Logo**: A geometric Flame or Spark.
    *   **Color Palette**: Orange, Red, Charcoal.

### Option B: CAPSULE (The "Structural" Theme) 💊
*   **Concept**: Sealed environments, futuristic transport, safety.
*   **Vibe**: Solid, reliable, medical/scientific precision.
*   **Ecosystem**:
    *   **CLI**: `cap` (Friendly, common word).
    *   **Daemon**: `capsuled` (Enclosed).
    *   **Config**: `Capsule.toml`.
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Logo**: A pill shape split diagonally, or a docking bay.
    *   **Color Palette**: White, Teal, Grey.

## 2. Logo Research
*   **Constraint**: The logo must be decomposable (Core shape for the ecosystem, sub-shapes for CLI/Daemon).
*   **Idea 1 (Ignite)**: Flame (Ecosystem) -> Spark (CLI) -> Coals (Daemon).
*   **Idea 2 (Capsule)**: Pill (Ecosystem) -> Lid (CLI) -> Container (Daemon).

## 3. Decision Status
*   Keeping both options open for the "Research" phase.
*   User leans towards these two over "Scientific/Mu" or "Speed/Blink".
